# 📋 EcoKids India — School Admin Playbook

**Month-by-month implementation guide for school administrators**

---

## 📅 Pre-Launch: Weeks 1-2

### Week 1: Account Setup & Verification

- [ ] **Receive login credentials** from EcoKids support
- [ ] **First login** → Verify school name, address, UDISE code
- [ ] **Upload school logo** (optional, for reports)
- [ ] **Set school timezone** (important for deadline calculations)
- [ ] **Explore the dashboard**: Familiarize yourself with all tabs

### Week 2: Import Student & Teacher Data

**Import from your school MIS or Excel:**

1. Go to **"Bulk Import"** → **"Upload CSV"**
2. Download **template file** (has correct column names)
3. Fill in your data:
   - Teachers: Name, Email, Classes taught (Grade X-Section A)
   - Students: Name, Email, Grade, Section, Roll Number, **Parent Email** (critical!)
4. Upload file → System validates → Confirms import

**At-a-glance Imports:**
- 500 students = 5 minutes
- 50 teachers = 2 minutes

**Troubleshooting:**
- Duplicate emails? System rejects & shows which rows
- Name format wrong? System suggests fix
- Missing parent emails? Can add later (but consent emails won't send)

---

## 🚀 Week 3: Launch — Student Orientation

### Send School-Wide Announcement
- Email all parents + students with:
  - EcoKids login link
  - Their username (email address)
  - **Temporary password** (system-generated)
  - "Your child can log in starting Monday!"
  - Include **STUDENT_QUICK_START.md** (download from docs)

### Conduct Student Assembly (15 minutes)
1. **What is EcoKids?** (2 min): "A platform where you earn points for environmental actions"
2. **Demo** (5 min): Show your phone → Log in → Submit mock activity → Show leaderboard
3. **Why it matters** (3 min): "Help us reach 1000 trees planted this year!"
4. **How to get started** (5 min): Students login on their phones (with IT help if needed)

### Teacher Training (30 minutes in staff room)
1. **Platform walkthrough** (10 min)
2. **Demo activity approval** (10 min)  
3. **Q&A** (10 min)
4. Provide **TEACHER_TRAINING_GUIDE.md** to all

## 📊 Month 1: Foundation (First 4 Weeks)

### Week 1: Initial Push
- [ ] **Send QR code cards** to students (generated in "QR Cards" section)
  - Print on cardstock or plastic sheets
  - Distribute to all students
  - Tell them: "Scan to login if you forget your password"

- [ ] **Run 2-3 teacher training sessions** (for early shift & late shift teachers separately)

- [ ] **Monitor first submissions**
  - Go to "Submissions" daily
  - Approve genuine submissions quickly (within 2 hours)
  - This builds momentum!

### Week 2: Fix Initial Issues  
- [ ] **Low parent email coverage?**
  - Run report: **"Students without parent email"**
  - Collect parent emails from teachers
  - Upload updated student list

- [ ] **Teachers struggling?**
  - Conduct individual tech support sessions
  - Assign EcoKids "champion teacher" who knows platform well

### Week 3-4: Build Momentum
- [ ] **Announce first school-wide mission**
  - E.g., "Plant 50 trees this month" or "Zero-waste lunch week"
  - Post on dashboard with countdown
  - Celebrate when milestone is reached

- [ ] **Share weekly leaderboard** with whole school
  - Print & post in staff room: "Top 5 students this week"
  - Celebrate in assembly

### End of Month Review
- [ ] Run **Monthly Report**:
  - Total eco-points earned: ____
  - Total activities submitted: ____
  - Total trees planted (approx): ____
  - Engagement rate: ____%
  - Share in staff meeting

## 📈 Month 2: Engagement

### Goal: Increase participation to 70%+

- [ ] **Collection parent consent**
  - Go to "Parent Consent Management"
  - Click "Send Bulk Consent Requests"
  - Parents receive email to approve child's participation
  - Track status in dashboard (see % per grade)

- [ ] **Establish weekly rhythm**
  - Every Monday: New mission posted (you create it)
  - Every Friday: Announce winners (assembly or email)
  - Every Sunday: Send parents/teachers a digest email

- [ ] **Create class competitions**
  - Grade 8-A vs Grade 8-B
  - Who can complete most activities?
  - Winning class @ lunch rewards

- [ ] **Teacher check-ins**
  - Ask: "Which students are struggling?"
  - EcoKids data shows: low engagement, zero submissions
  - Pair struggling students with engaged buddies

### Mid-Month Pulse Check
- [ ] **Send survey to students**: "What activities do you want to see?"
  - Get feedback
  - Adjust missions accordingly

---

## 🎯 Month 3: Sustainability Goals

### Goal: Lock in habits, prepare for formal reporting

- [ ] **Set NEP 2020 targets** (New Education Policy aligned)
  - System tracks 6 competencies: Environmental Awareness, Critical Thinking, Sustainability Skills, etc.
  - Goal: 80% of class meeting each competency by year-end
  - Create missions aligned to weak competencies

- [ ] **Deep-dive into analytics**
  - Go to "Analytics" → "NEP Competency Report"
  - See: Which students need help with which topics
  - Recommend teachers assign related quizzes

- [ ] **Prepare impact report**
  - EcoKids dashboard shows: trees planted, CO₂ prevented, water saved
  - Prepare 1-page summary for principal
  - Include: "Our school's environmental impact this quarter"

- [ ] **Parent engagement event**
  - Invite parents to school
  - Show EcoKids demo + student achievements
  - Survey parents: "How can we increase participation?"

### End of Quarter Review
- [ ] **Generate official report**:
  - Go to **"Reports" → "Quarterly Summary"**
  - Download PDF
  - Share with principal & staff
  - File for annual compliance audit

## 🆘 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Students can't login"
- Check: Did you upload student list with email addresses?
- Did students complete first-time setup (confirming grade)?
- Provide **QR backup login** if email isn't working

### "Very low engagement (< 30%)"
- Common cause: lack of teacher assignments
- Solution: Conduct another teacher training
- Make it mandatory: "Approve one activity per student per week"

### "Teachers say it takes too long to approve activities"
- Teaching point: Average approval = 30 seconds (use mobile app!)
- Batch approvals: Set aside 10 min daily rather than checking randomly
- Streamline: Reject obviously fake photos immediately, don't overthink

### "Parent emails not working / Consent not increasing"
- Check: Did you upload parent emails correctly?
- Resend consent requests: Go to "Bulk Send" again (send reminders)
- Alternative: Collect consent on paper → manually mark in system

### "Teachers report missing NEP training"
- Use: **TEACHER_TRAINING_GUIDE.md** (link in dashboard)
- Host monthly Office Hours (15 min each, Q&A)
- Create WhatsApp group for quick questions

### "Can't export student data / Print reports"
- Reports on dashboard are CSV-downloadable
- Go to "Analytics" → click **"Export"** button
- If button missing: Check your role (must be SCHOOL_ADMIN)

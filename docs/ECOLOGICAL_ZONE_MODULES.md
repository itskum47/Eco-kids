# Ecological Zone Modules

This document formalizes five ecological learning modules for India-aligned contextual education.

## Module 1: Himalayan (8 lessons)
1. Alpine Ecosystems
2. Water Cycle in Mountains
3. Landslide Risk and Prevention
4. Tourism Impact on Mountains
5. Medicinal Plants of Himalayas
6. Mountain Conservation Efforts
7. Climate Change in Himalayas
8. Community Action: High-Altitude Plantation

## Module 2: Coastal (8 lessons)
1. Marine Ecosystems
2. Coral Reefs and Biodiversity
3. Mangrove Forests
4. Marine Plastic Pollution
5. Fishing and Overharvesting
6. Coastal Erosion and Climate
7. Beach Cleanup Action
8. Ocean Conservation Projects

## Module 3: Desert (7 lessons)
1. Desert Ecosystems
2. Water Scarcity and Management
3. Desertification: Causes and Solutions
4. Renewable Energy in Deserts
5. Desert Agriculture
6. Wildlife Adaptation
7. Community Solutions for Arid Regions

## Module 4: Forest (8 lessons)
1. Forest Types and Biodiversity
2. Deforestation and Impact
3. Wildlife Habitats
4. Forest Restoration
5. Indigenous Knowledge Systems
6. Carbon Sequestration
7. Community Forests
8. Reforestation Action Lab

## Module 5: Urban (7 lessons)
1. Urban Heat Islands
2. Air Quality and Pollution
3. Waste Management in Cities
4. Green Spaces and Parks
5. Sustainable Transport
6. Water in Cities
7. Urban Gardening Action

## Implementation Notes

- Each lesson should have bilingual content and one practical activity.
- Every module should include at least one local data collection or school-community action.
- Assessments should map to SDG and curriculum matrix entries.

# 🌱 EcoKids India — Student Quick Start (One-Page Guide)

Ready to become an Eco-Champion? Here's everything you need to know in 5 minutes!

---

## 🚀 How to Login

**For students WITH email:**
1. Go to **ecokids.in**
2. Click **"Student Login"**
3. Enter your email & password
4. Done! 🎉

**For students WITHOUT email:**
1. Your teacher gives you a **QR card** (printed card with a square code)
2. Scan it with your phone camera
3. Follow the link → auto-login!
4. No password needed!

---

## 🎯 How to Earn Eco-Points

Every action = Eco-Points = Rewards!

### Activity Submission (10-50 points)
**"I planted a tree"** or **"I segregated waste"**

Steps:
1. Click **"Submit Activity"**
2. Choose activity type (Tree planting, Waste segregation, Water conservation, etc.)
3. Take a clear **photo** (show the activity & yourself)
4. Add a caption: "We planted this tree at school"
5. Click **"Submit"**
6. Your teacher reviews it (⏳ takes 1-2 days)
7. ✅ Approved = Points instantly added!

### Quiz (5-20 points)
**"Completed a quiz about climate change"**

Steps:
1. Click **"Quizzes"**
2. Pick a quiz on any topic you like
3. Answer all questions
4. ✅ Submitted = Points instantly!
5. Check your score

### Weekly Missions (30-100 points)
**"Complete this week's challenge"**

Examples:
- "Plant 3 trees by Friday" (80 points)
- "Reduce plastic for 3 days" (50 points)
- "Score ≥75% on environment quiz" (60 points)

Steps:
1. See mission on your dashboard
2. Complete the challenge
3. Submit proof (photo)
4. Done!

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Why is my activity still pending?**
A: Teacher is reviewing it. Usually takes 1-2 days. Clear photos = faster approval!

**Q: What if my activity gets rejected?**
A: Teacher will explain why (e.g., "Photo doesn't show the activity clearly"). Resubmit with a better photo!

**Q: Can I work with my friends?**
A: Yes! Some activities allow group submissions. You **all earn points together**!

**Q: How do I see the global leaderboard?**
A: Go to "Leaderboard" → Toggle to "All Schools" (if your school allows it). See where your school ranks in India!

**Q: What happens after I get Level 10?**
A: You become an **Eco-Champion**! Get special badge + can mentor other students. Keep earning points for milestone rewards!

**Q: Can parents see my progress?**
A: Yes! Go to ecokids.in → Click "Parent Report" → Your parents can see: eco-points, badges, activities, quiz scores, and your environmental impact!
